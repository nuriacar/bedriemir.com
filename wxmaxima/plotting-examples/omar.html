Omar Hayyam Method

This plot explains Omar Khayyam's graphical method to solve the cubic equation x3+a2x=b, for a,b>0:
(http://riotorto.users.sourceforge.net/Maxima/gnuplot/omar/index.html)

(Realised with wxmaxima rel. 17.10.1)
